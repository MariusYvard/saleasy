# Account growth (post-sale)

Grow revenue from existing customers: expansion, renewal and referrals. A customer must realize value before you push more.

## Expansion triggers
Scan each account for seven signals: usage near a ceiling, a feature gap, a new department with the same pain, customer growth, a strategic initiative your product supports, a competitive tool to consolidate, a services need. Score each opportunity on revenue, effort and likelihood and work the high-revenue, low-effort, high-likelihood ones first.

## Renewal risk
Score upcoming renewals on health signals: usage trend, engagement, support load, champion presence (a departed champion is a major flag), value realized. Band green, yellow or red. For yellow and red, write a save plan (re-establish value, re-engage or replace the champion, resolve the open issue, book a review) before the renewal date. Never let a red renewal arrive as a surprise.

## Referrals
Sources: happy customers first, then partners and network champions. Ask at the referral moment, within about 48 hours of a stated win. Be specific: name the title or company type you want introduced, not "anyone who comes to mind". Run a three-touch track and always close the loop with the referrer. Track asks and outcomes in the CRM or `saleasy-pipeline`.
