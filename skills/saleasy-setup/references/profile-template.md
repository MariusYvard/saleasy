# Saleasy profile template

Copy this structure to `saleasy-profile.md`. Replace bracketed values. Leave `(à compléter)` where unknown. Every Saleasy skill reads this file.

```markdown
# Saleasy profile

Updated: [YYYY-MM-DD]

## Identity
- Name: [name]
- Role: [role]
- Profile type: [founder | SDR/BDR | AE | full-cycle | marketer | agency]
- Company: [company]

## Offer
- What I sell: [one or two sentences]
- Price band: [self-serve | mid-market | enterprise]

## Target
- Industry: [...]
- Company size: [...]
- Geography: [...]
- Buyer persona (titles): [...]
- Buyer pains: [...]

## Differentiation
- Why us: [1-2 reasons]
- Main competitors: [...]

## Motion
- Type: [inbound | outbound | both]
- Sales cycle: [length]
- Weekly targets: [leads, meetings, ...]

## Voice
- Tone (outbound): [...]
- Tone (content): [...]
- Languages: [...]
- Never say: [...]

## Tools and sending
- Email: [tool or none]
- Calendar: [tool or none]
- CRM: [tool or pipeline file]
- Lead data: [tool or none]
- Social: [tool or none]
- Spreadsheet: [tool or none]
- Call notes: [tool or none]
- Channels authorized for sending after validation: [...]
- Automated monitoring: [on | off]
- Open-data pack (France): [on | off]
- INSEE Sirene token: [token or none]

## Proof assets
- Case studies / metrics: [...]
- Testimonials: [...]
- Email signature: [...]
- Booking link: [...]
```

## Profile-type presets
- Founder or solo: motion both, prioritize selling (briefing) and prospecting.
- SDR or BDR: motion outbound, high volume, prioritize prospecting, target meetings booked.
- Account Executive: motion closing, prioritize selling, target pipeline coverage and forecast.
- Full-cycle: all skills, balance prospecting and selling.
- Marketer: prioritize marketing, target reach and pipeline influenced.
- Agency or freelance: multiple client profiles possible; store a separate `saleasy-profile-[client].md` per client and ask which to use at the start of a task.
